Making a claim for loss of notice

Based on the dates you’ve given, your statutory notice period is from 01 March 2015 to 30 April 2015.

You’ll receive a letter or email asking you to complete your claim after 30 April 2015. The letter or email may take up to 6 weeks to arrive.

If you don’t claim for Jobseeker's Allowance your payment for loss of notice will be affected.

What you can do next
